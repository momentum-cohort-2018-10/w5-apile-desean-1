About Linkinator

Linkinator is a place where you can find the most popular links across the web. Links are voted on by users, and the best links rise to the top. Reddit, eat your heart out! -- ok maybe not...

Linkinator was created by Deanna Jacobs and Sean Houde as a project in Momentum Learn's Immersive Python/Django coding course.
